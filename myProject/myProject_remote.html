Welcome to Pacman Pinball! You are able to control the triangle and rectangle shapes and the speed of the windmill.

The goal is to get the pacman to the cherries!

Don't panic if there is lag, it's to make sure you can see the game in action when you are switching between the website and Zoom.

Once you are ready to hit your shot, choose how hard you want to hit it and it will shoot!
